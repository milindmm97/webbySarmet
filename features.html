This indigenous optical design helped build a cheap and affordable solution for a See Through Head Mounted Display. SARmet prototype shown has a proof of concept design of this Optical system. Our Team is close to miniaturizing this setup to be integrated into a product

Intelligent Rear Camera

SARmet will feature a wide angle 120 degree FOV rear camera. This camera streams the rear view to the Head Mounted Display and also warns the rider regarding any approaching speeding vehicles. This helps keep the rider situationally aware.

Connectivity

SARmet has a dedicated mobile app which connects to SARmet via Wifi and Bluetooth. Via this connection, SARmet helps you manage your calls and messages, get your navigational information all in your field of view via the Head Mounted Display
